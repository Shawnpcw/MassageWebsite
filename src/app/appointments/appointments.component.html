Relaxation Massage

Relaxation massage, which is the same as a Swedish massage, has many benefits when it comes to stress, immune system, relaxation, and all around reboosting your body back to its homotosis state. Relaxation massage helps with stress because it can lower your heart rate and reduce levels of insulin and cortisol which helps boost your immune system. The massage techniques that go along promote the body’s outer tissue to promote relaxation of the mind and body. Relaxation massage also releases your body’s hormones that are responsible for your overall well-being, dopamine and serotonin, which kick your brain back into high gear. When it comes to relaxation massage, the reason to choose it is if you need to reset your self. If you are having pain then you should choose treatment massage.

Treatment Massage

Treatment massage, also known as deep tissue, sports massage, or therapeutic massage, all help with if you are feeling any pain. Like relaxation massage, it has many positive effects on your body. It can help manage chronic pain, it can also help with stress, as well as improve any athletic performance you may need. Treatment massage helps set your muscles back to the level that they should be, without tightness caused by stress or injury. It helps manage pain by releasing dopamine that is your body’s own pain killer. The reason to get a treatment massage is if you are training for a marathon, working out, or you sit and stare at a computer screen all day.
